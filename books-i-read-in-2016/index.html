Books I Read in 2016

Here is the list of books I read in 2016, roughly in the order I read them. I started out the first half of the year on Kindle for iPhone, getting through Sapiens, but mid-year I switched to listening to audiobooks while working out. Wow! Completely blown away at how much more efficient it is to consume books this way and it is one of the most powerful life pro-tips I can recommend. Some people even listen at 2x speed, or higher!
